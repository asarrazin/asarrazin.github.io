Notable School Projects

at Bowdoin College

  • Museum Navigation Mobile Application - An application for visually impaired individuals that sought to assist in navigating museums independently. Designed and coded the mobile application in Visual Studio using C#. Connected to a database holding information of artwork and the museum gallery.
  • Predictive Keyboard - A predictive word generator that uses the same principles that the human brain uses to make predicitions, employing word association models, grammar rules, and sentence topic detection. Incorporates context analysis and word association as well as basic language principles to produce word predictions that are more accurate and helpful than those produced by simple statistical mechanisms. Built using Java. ( Github).
  • Let's Talk About Sex Baby - The Ethics and Impact of Sex Robots - An ethics paper that explores sex robots, its current industry, and the moral drawbacks that come with the creation and implementation of these robots in society.
